WELCOME

The Gumala Trust has a vital role as the Trustee of the General Gumala Foundation, and as such, has legal responsibilities to appropriately manage and supervise the monies received and distributed on behalf of the Foundation under the terms of its Trust Deed. Since 1997, The Gumala Trust has been acting as a ‘safe pair of hands’ to ensure the best interest of our Beneficiaries, the Traditional Owners of the Yinhawangka, Bunjima and Nyiyaparli people, are being met both now and in the future...
